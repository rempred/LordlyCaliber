# VGMaps Scenario Map Art

This folder bundles the full-art *Ogre Battle 64: Person of Lordly Caliber*
mission maps used by the Scenario tab's calibrated map view.

The PNGs were copied from the parent research workspace's
`wiki/maps/vgmaps/` harvest. They originate from
[VGMaps.com](https://www.vgmaps.com/) (The Video Game Atlas), where they were
ripped and contributed by the VGMaps community. Bundled here with attribution
and permission for the editor's scenario map display and calibration workflow.

The smaller in-game map variants from the research harvest remain in the parent
research workspace; the editor loads the full-art PNG filenames referenced by
`scenario-map-calibration.js`.
